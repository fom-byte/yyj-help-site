Room Assignment Tips

  • Read Alerts & Notes in Shift Reports before assigning.
  • Upgrade Diamond guests: NQQ2→S12, ENQ2→ENQ3, NQ1/ENQ1→S11.
  • If oversold, sort by price and upgrade highest-paying, shortest-stay guests first.
  • Don't put kids on 3rd or 4th floor NQQ2s.
  • For NQQ2s: highest-paying on 4th/3rd floor.
  • For S12s: assign 118–128 by rate, save 103 & 105 for near-lobby requests or lowest-rate guests.
  • SNQ2 and NK1: higher-rate guests in lower room numbers (reverse of normal).
  • Fill requests first, then prioritize by rate, then direct bookings.
  • Assign clean/checked-out rooms before occupied rooms for early arrivals.
  • Do NOT use auto-assign until all special-needs and multi-night guests are assigned.
  • Airline room change: write reason in blue book.

NQQ2 vs NQQ1 — Room Moves

NQQ1 and NQQ2 rooms are identical in quality. NQQ1 faces the back wall and is pet-designated.
  • NQQ1 rooms are not bookable online (except last-minute when opened by management).
  • Housekeeping follows strict pet cleaning after any pet stay. Only ~4–5 pet stays/month.
  • Guests paying lowest rates (OTAs, trucking, construction, coast guard) commonly assigned NQQ1.
  • If a guest complains about wall view or pet smell and NQQ2 is available, move them. Offer S12 or ENQ2 upgrade for a fee.
  • If a guest has allergies or pet sensitivities, add to reservation alerts and profile notes.

WiFi

Guest Login

  1. Connect to network: YYJDaysInn (not "Days Inn Admin")
  2. Enter room number and last name (or company name for airline rooms)
  3. Most phones/tablets will auto-launch the sign-in page
  4. PC users may need to open a browser and go to a standard HTTP page (e.g. shaw.ca)

Troubleshooting Steps

  1. Verify the correct network (YYJDaysInn, not Days Inn Admin)
  2. Check spelling of last name
  3. Try "forget network" and reconnect
  4. Have guest go to logout.now to clear credentials, then reconnect
  5. Generate an Authentication Code via the WiFi Code Printer — enter room number to match length of stay
If multiple guests are calling about WiFi at once, call Shaw to fix the issue (not just individual troubleshooting).

Making Reservations

Normal Reservation

  1. Bookings → Reservations → Look to Book
  2. Select dates, number of people → Search
  3. Select rate (always try rack first: RROD) → Select
  4. Click New Profile (or search for returning guest via magnify glass)
  5. Enter Last Name, First Name, Phone, Email, Address, Postal Code
  6. Save and Select Profile
  7. Enter Credit Card # and Expiration
  8. Full read-back of stay info with guest
  9. Book Now
  10. Add any alerts or fixed charges
  11. Confirmation Letter → Email → Send
Full Read-Back includes: check-in/out dates, room type and rate, people in room, cancellation policy, check-in/out times. Type FRB 24hr cxl every time you do a full read-back.

Company Reservation

  1. Bookings → Reservations → Look to Book
  2. Select dates, people, rooms
  3. Enter company name in COMPANY field → tab
  4. Click + beside company → Notes — check for PO requirements, direct bill info, room type instructions
  5. Select company → Search
  6. Select negotiated rate if available → Select
  7. New Profile or search for returning guest
  8. Enter guest info, CC (or see direct bill instructions)
  9. Full read-back → Book Now → Add alerts/charges → Confirmation email
Always put company names in the COMPANY field. Government discounts also require the branch in the company spot.

Park & Fly Reservation

Park & Fly is available October 1 – March 31 only. Must be booked directly through the hotel. Always 7-day cancellation policy.
  1. Check Parking Availability chart — max 20 spots
  2. Bookings → Reservations → Look to Book
  3. Select dates, people, rooms
  4. Under Company type "Park & Fly" → Select → Search
  5. Select Park & Fly rate → follow normal reservation steps
  6. In Alerts: note length of parking and shuttle times
  7. Add guest to the Parking Availability chart
  8. Inform guest they must leave keys in a sealed envelope at front desk

Returning keys: Must verify guest's ID matches the name or address on the envelope.

If a guest stays one night on either end of the trip, they get 14 days of parking included at the P&F rate. Extra days beyond 7 without a stay: $10/day + taxes.

Check-in

Guest Policies

Guide Dog & Service Dog Certification

Legal Rights

Service dogs must be allowed anywhere the public is normally permitted, including all hotel areas.

Businesses cannot charge extra fees or restrict access. So no Pet Fees.

Verification

If it is not obvious that a dog is a service dog, staff may:

  • Politely ask if the dog is a guide or service dog.
  • Request to see the official BC Guide Dog & Service Dog Certification Card.

Certification isn’t required, but a BC government ID card is the easiest way to confirm a dog is a service animal.

Staff may not ask about the person’s disability.

Emotional Support Animals

Not eligible for certification under the Act.

Do not have the same legal protections as service dogs.

References

  • BC Guide Dog and Service Dog Act (BC Laws)
  • Guide Dog and Service Dog Certification – Province of BC
  • Fact Sheet: Service & Guide Dogs – Government of BC (PDF)

More Info

More info on Service Animal vs Emotional Support

If a Guest Disputes the Policy

  • Stay calm and professional.
  • Explain that service animals are protected under BC law and are not subject to pet fees.
  • Use the BC Guide Dog and Service Dog Act references above if needed.
  • If the situation continues, note it in the log and escalate to a manager.

If Staff Are Unsure

  • Ask if it is a guide or service dog.
  • If needed, request to see the BC certification card.
  • If the guest confirms it is a service dog, allow access and document the situation if needed.
