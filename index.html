Special Features & Pets

Pets

Service animals are not considered pets and are not subject to pet fees.

  • Allowed in designated pet-friendly rooms on the first floor (alley side)
  • Pet fee applies

Guide Dog & Service Dog Certification Policy

Guide Dog & Service Dog Certification

Decision Tree (Staff Use)

  • Is the dog clearly a service dog?
    • Yes → Allow access. No pet fee. No further questions.
    • No → Proceed below
  • Ask: “Is this a guide or service dog?”
    • Yes → Proceed below
    • No → Treat as pet (pet policy applies)
  • Request certification (optional):
    • Ask to see BC Guide/Service Dog ID card
    • If provided → Allow, no fee
    • If not provided → Still allow if guest confirms
  • Important Rules:
    • Do NOT ask about the guest’s disability
    • Do NOT deny access
    • Do NOT charge pet fees
  • Emotional Support Animal?
    • Not legally a service animal
    • Pet policy applies

Troubleshooting

Service Animal Disputes

Quick Answer

  • Service dogs must be allowed
  • No pet fees apply
  • Do not ask about disability

If Guest Challenges Policy

  • Stay calm and professional
  • Explain: “Service animals are protected under BC law and are not subject to pet fees”
  • If needed, reference BC Guide Dog & Service Dog Act

If Staff Are Unsure

  • Ask if it is a service dog
  • Optionally request certification
  • If still unclear → allow access and note in log

Escalation

  • If situation becomes difficult → contact manager
